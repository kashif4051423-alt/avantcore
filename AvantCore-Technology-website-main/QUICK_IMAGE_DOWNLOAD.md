## Tips for Best Results

- Download images at **1200x800px** or larger
- Compress images before uploading (use TinyPNG.com)
- Look for **professional, corporate-style** images
- Avoid cartoon or overly stylized images
- Ensure **good quality** and **clear visibility**
- Prefer images with **minimal text** overlays
