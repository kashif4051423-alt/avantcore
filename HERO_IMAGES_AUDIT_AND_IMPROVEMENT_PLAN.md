# Hero Background Images Audit & Improvement Plan

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive audit of all hero background images across the website and detailed recommendations for unique, premium enterprise imagery that directly represents page content and business purpose.

---

## AUDIT FINDINGS

### Current Problems Identified

1. **Generic Stock Photography**: Many pages use standard Unsplash images that could fit any tech company
2. **Repetitive Themes**: Multiple pages share similar "office meeting" or "people at desk" compositions
3. **Misaligned Content**: Hero images don't specifically represent the page's business purpose
4. **Lack of Visual Hierarchy**: No distinctive visual identity per page type
5. **Professional Quality Gaps**: Some images lack the premium enterprise magazine quality needed

---

## Recommended Image Sources (Premium Quality)

### Primary Sources:
1. **Unsplash** (Curated Premium Selection)
   - Filter for: Professional photography, enterprise environments, tech spaces
   - Search terms: "enterprise", "operations center", "data center", "modern office", "tech innovation"

2. **Pexels** (High-Quality Professional)
   - Similar premium business photography
   - Enterprise and tech-focused collections

3. **Pixabay** (Business & Technology)
   - Professional business and technology imagery
   - High resolution options

4. **Custom Photography** (Recommended for Premium Brand)
   - Consider commissioning unique professional photography
   - Specific to AVANTCORE's business positioning
   - Industry-specific settings and environments

### NOT Recommended:
- Generic stock sites (Getty, Shutterstock without careful curation)
- AI-generated images
- Cartoon/illustration styles
- Images with text/logos/watermarks
- Obvious stock photo patterns

---

## Image Technical Specifications

### Format & Quality:
- Format: JPEG or WebP (optimized)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum (for hero sections)
- File Size: Optimized to < 500KB per image
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (standard hero section)
- Color Profile: sRGB (web-safe)

### Styling Requirements:
- Cinematic lighting and composition
- Professional color grading
- Modern, clean aesthetic
- No distracting elements
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Ready for text overlay (good contrast for hero text)

## VISUAL CONSISTENCY GUIDELINES

### DO:
- ✅ Use cinematic lighting and professional composition
- ✅ Include technology/innovation elements that relate to page topic
- ✅ Use professional people in modern business environments (when appropriate)
- ✅ Emphasize modern, clean workspaces
- ✅ Show enterprise operations/solutions in action
- ✅ Use AVANTCORE brand colors subtly (blues, whites)
- ✅ Create visual storytelling through environment and composition

### DON'T:
- ❌ Repeat the same office/meeting room scene
- ❌ Use generic "stock photo" looking images
- ❌ Include cartoons, illustrations, or AI art
- ❌ Show unrelated content (e.g., generic nature/sky backgrounds)
- ❌ Use low-resolution or compressed images
- ❌ Add text, logos, or watermarks to images
- ❌ Show clichéd success imagery (thumbs up, high fives, etc.)
- ❌ Use the same composition repeated on multiple pages
